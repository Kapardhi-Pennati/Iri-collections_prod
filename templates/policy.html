Return & Refund Policy

All Sales Are Final

At Iri Collections, we take great pride in the quality and craftsmanship of our jewelry. To ensure that every customer receives a pristine and unworn piece, we adhere to a strict policy regarding returns, refunds, and exchanges.

No Returns, Refunds, or Exchanges

Once a product is sold and the order is confirmed, all sales are final. We do not accept returns, issue refunds, or allow exchanges for any products purchased through our store.

Quality Assurance

Before any item is shipped, it undergoes a thorough quality control inspection to ensure it meets our high standards. We guarantee that the item you receive is exactly as described and shown on our website.

If you have any questions or concerns about a specific piece before making a purchase, please feel free to reach out to our support team. We are more than happy to provide additional details, measurements, or photos to help you make an informed decision.
